Parsley Tea

I am drinking parsley tea,
the one from your mom,
She thinks ”we are friends”
And I am used to drinking from an ordinary mug,

It is funny, is not it?
As the simplest things are important
Like a cup of tea,
Porcelain, glass and plastic
Does not mean anything, without an embedded meaning
The contents inside do not change the significance of the glass

Is it half empty or full
of what you want? Juice, tea, lemonade?
I do not want anything,
I miss those moments insanely

The same.. in a mug
I want tea with parsley
So that you and your mother are next
to me and continue to think “we are friends”
Because this is how it should be

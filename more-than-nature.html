More Than Nature

Dark days and wet asphalt
follow me uninvited.
And the sounds are unpleasant: there is a viola,
There is no soprano at all,

And not to say that, unfortunately
The dark forest is so beautiful
and the sky is grey, that pours down tears on us, it is also sad.
it did not choose its colour and shape,
and all hate it for what it is.

This is unfair,
although there are those who love the grey and they are inspired like that:
when the clouds are sad in nature
on the very rude note
And when they happily sit.

We need to appreciate and love
all the things that we take for granted,
indeed, in one moment it can just so happen
that everything will cease to be.
